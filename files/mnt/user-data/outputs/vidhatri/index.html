What is Bio Enzyme?

Nature's Most
Powerful Cleaner

A bio enzyme is a natural protein catalyst produced by living organisms — bacteria, fungi, or plants — through fermentation. These microscopic molecular machines break down complex organic molecules like fats, proteins, and starches into harmless, simple compounds.

Unlike synthetic chemical cleaners that attack surfaces with harsh alkalis and acids, bio enzymes work with precision — targeting specific types of dirt, grease, and bacteria. They leave no toxic residue, don't irritate skin, and fully biodegrade in waterways within days.

At Vidhatri, we cultivate bio enzymes primarily from rescued citrus peels — rich in natural citric acid and enzymatic compounds — combined with fruit sugars and beneficial bacteria over a controlled fermentation process of 90+ days.

🧬

What Makes Bio Enzyme Special

Traditional cleaners kill everything indiscriminately — including beneficial microbes. Bio enzymes are smart: they only target the specific organic matter causing the problem, leaving healthy microbiomes intact.

⚗️

Fermentation Science

Our enzymes are fermented for 90+ days with citrus peels, jaggery and water in precise ratios.

🔬

Molecular Precision

Each enzyme type targets a specific molecule — lipase for fats, protease for protein, amylase for starch.

🌱

Living Chemistry

Active bio cultures continue working even after application, breaking down residue over time.

💧

Aquatic Safe

Fully biodegrades within 3–7 days in water systems. No aquatic toxicity whatsoever.

The Science

How Bio Enzyme Works

From fermentation to your sink — a 5-stage journey of natural cleaning science.

🍋
Ingredient Rescue

Lemon peels, orange rinds, and other citrus waste are collected before they reach the landfill.

🍯
Fermentation Mix

Citrus is combined with jaggery (sugar) and water in a 1:3:10 ratio and sealed for 90 days.

🧫
Enzyme Cultivation

Beneficial bacteria and yeast naturally present in citrus metabolise the sugars and produce enzyme-rich liquid.

🫧
Product Crafting

Strained enzyme liquid is blended with plant-based co-ingredients to create each specific product.

✨
Clean & Safe

Applied to surfaces, enzymes break down grease, bacteria and stains. No toxic residue left behind.

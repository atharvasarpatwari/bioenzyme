How to Get the
Best Results

1
Use Warm Water

Warm water activates the citric bio-enzymes, helping them break down grease and food residue more effectively.

2
Less is More

Vidhatri is highly concentrated — just 2–3 drops per wash cycle is enough. Avoid overuse to get the best value per bottle.

3
Pre-Soak Heavy Grease

For heavily soiled pots and pans, add a drop directly to the dish with a little water and let it soak for 3–5 minutes before scrubbing.

4
Store at Room Temperature

Keep your bottle away from direct sunlight and heat to preserve the potency of the natural enzymes. Use within 12 months of opening.

5
Safe for All Surfaces

Vidhatri is pH-balanced for use on ceramic, glass, stainless steel, non-stick cookware, and even delicate crystal dishware.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is Vidhatri safe for children and babies? +
Absolutely. Our formula uses only food-grade botanical ingredients with no synthetic chemicals, making it completely safe for washing baby bottles, feeding equipment, and household dishes that children use. Always rinse thoroughly as with any cleaning product.
How does it compare to regular dish soap? +
Vidhatri is significantly more concentrated than commercial dish soaps, which means you use less per wash. It's free from the harsh sulfates and synthetic fragrances found in conventional products, yet delivers comparable — often superior — cleaning performance especially on greasy items.
Can it be used in a dishwasher? +
Vidhatri is formulated for hand-washing dishes. It creates a rich lather that is not suitable for dishwasher machines (which require low-suds detergents). We're currently developing a dishwasher-compatible version — stay tuned!
How long does one bottle last? +
Due to the concentrated formula, one standard 500ml bottle typically lasts a family of 4 approximately 3–5 weeks with regular daily use. This makes Vidhatri excellent value compared to conventional dish liquids of the same volume.
Do you offer bulk or subscription orders? +

What is your delivery area? +
We currently deliver across all major cities in India including Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, and more. Delivery to your door typically takes 1–3 business days depending on your location.
